About Us

Fairies, brownies, unicorns - everything exists if you believe in it. We present you a unique chance to find an unusual friend. To become a happy owner of a unicorn, you only need to fill out a questionnaire and we will contact you. But not everything is so simple, do not forget that you do not choose the unicorn, but the unicorn chooses you. Before you can take the unicorn home, you need to take a three-month course that will prepare you and the unicorn for the future. You will learn how to take care of unicorns, what they like to do in their free time, how often it is necessary to arrange a day of laziness and become real friends with your future pet.
